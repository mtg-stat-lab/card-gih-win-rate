‚è≤Ô∏è Timer Focus Test (Internal Focus)

Goal: Detect mental drift during a simple internal task.

Steps:

  • Set a 30-second timer (phone face down or hidden).
  • Sit comfortably. Close your eyes.
  • Focus only on counting seconds steadily in your mind:
    • "one... two... three..." ‚Äî no breathing tricks, no body cues.
  • When you believe 30 seconds have passed, open your eyes.

Pass Criteria:

  • No distracting thoughts.
  • No "falling asleep" mid-count.
  • Calm, smooth counting experience.

If fail: Attention is fragmented. Reset before queuing.

üõ¨ Visual Object Grounding Test (External Focus)

Goal: Detect mental drift while observing external detail.

Steps:

  • Look around and silently name 5 objects you can see (e.g., "lamp, chair, desk").
  • Pick one object (e.g., a lamp).
  • Spend 30 seconds focusing completely on that object:
    • Observe colors, shapes, textures.
    • Notice small details without letting your mind wander.

Pass Criteria:

  • No thought drift away from the object.
  • No impatience or internal storytelling.
  • Calm, steady external attention.

If fail: Attention not anchored. Reset before queuing.
